Anapest News

A live-project about scraping CNN articles and formatting them into anapest poems.
This process is done through an algorithm that iterates through each word and removes any words that conflict with the anapest meter. Then, certain grammar rules are enforced, as well as rules that add syntax and stanzas. A server generates these poems using CRON job scheduler. Poems are generated a 6AM, noon, and 6PM, and are hosted on this website, designed like the original articles. You can read today's poems below!
